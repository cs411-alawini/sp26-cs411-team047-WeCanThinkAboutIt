## Project Summary

We want to alleviate uncertainty and stress for students considering employment options/opportunities in their majors. A lot of students that pick STEM majors assume a baseline level of employment that comes with the major but that can change depending on the industry. Our product is intended to be open to all students who are looking for information about particular career paths and job roles based on their expertise. Our users will be able to enter their major, university, location, and relevant sectors they want to pursue. With that information they will be able to see an index of Job roles/positions (with relevant information like salary, work-life balance (low,medium,high), etc) that would best match their major. The job will also highlight required skills that the user is missing to be competitive and redirect the opportunities available at their particular university.
