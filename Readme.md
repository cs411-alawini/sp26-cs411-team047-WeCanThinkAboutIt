# Team047-WeCanThinkAboutIt

## Summary:
GradPath is a database web application designed to alleviate the uncertainty and stress for students that do not have a clear picture of possible career choices and employment opportunities within their expertise. Many students come into college with the notion that a job is lined up once they graduate, which cannot be further from the truth depending on the competitiveness of the job market. Employment opportunities change all the time and vary drastically from region, current economic level, graduation cycle, and industry trends. 
With this in mind, our application is designed to integrate all of these analytics into one platform that produces a transparent view on specific career paths based on user preferences. Users will enter their major, degree level, university, and other criteria which will help our system produce unemployment rate statistics, salary trends, and overall job stability indicators in specific industries/sectors and regions. A student's education is also considered since higher level education is also another option for most students looking to pursue higher paying job roles. This is functionally different from a job board as this platform is not designed to host job opportunities but to help point students and other users towards job titles they should be pursuing and also provide industry analytics based on their major and degree level. The platform is designed with the intention to help students evaluate their current career trajectory and make them reassess if they should pursue different interior paths with the help of GradPath’s suggestions and data visualizations.
